How to make a launch template:
-click on Instances on the left under Instances> click on your instance you made> click on Actions> image and templates> create template from instance
-create launch template> launch template name and description> launch template name: homepageagain> paste the name in the description
-scroll down to check if everything is correct> click on create launch template

How to connect to your instance:
-click on your instance you made> click connect
-EC2 Instance Connect> connect using a public ip clicked and click on Connect
-ctrl+z to stop the ping
-ctrl+z to start

How to make two instances:
-click on instance you made> click on instance state: terminate
-launch instance from template
-select source template: select a launch template
-scroll down to Summary on the right and put 2
-launch instance
-go to ec2 and now running two instances
